AGIPOP

AGIPOP is a Plaster of Paris Bandage made up of Leno Gauze and finest quality Plaster Of Paris powder. AGIPOP is indicated for the use mostly in FractureS for immobilization. It can also be used in making slabs (splints). AGIPOP is available in rolls as well as slabs form.

Features:
Fast Setting Time
Excellent Strength
Low Powder Loss
White color cast
More Powder (GSM)

DUROSOFT

AGICREPE

AGICREPE © is Cotton Crepe Bandage made from highly twisted cotton crepe yarn.
AGICREPE offers uniform compression which enhances blood circulation.
AGICREPE is used as compression bandage for better blood circulation, swelling, strain, sprain, varicose veins etc.
Features:
Excellent Elasticity & Regain properties
Uniform compression
Flash Color
Washable
Fast Edges
